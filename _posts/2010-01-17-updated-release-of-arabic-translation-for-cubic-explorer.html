A year ago, I released an Arabic translation for my favorite alternative of Windows Explorer, Cubic Explorer. After a year of updates of the software, I thought the translation might need some updating and I also noticed some mistakes that needed to be fixed.

Now you can get an updated release of the translation (release 1.1). You can find it at my public SkyDrive here. This translation works with Cubic Explorer release 0.91.0.1223 (SVN Snapshot) which you can get here.

Hopefully the updated translation will be included in the installer very soon.

Enjoy.
